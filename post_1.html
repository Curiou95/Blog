Leadership & Personal development

Posted on March 3, 2024 by matende
Lifestyle Habits

In our fast-paced world, effective leadership and personal development are more critical than ever. Aspiring leaders seek guidance on how to navigate challenges, and that is why Refactory Uganda found it prudent to teach this new cohort of future tech moguls on this princple.

With none other than the book called The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen Covey. The seven habits seek to move one from being a dependant to someone who is interdepenant.

Habit 1: Be Proactive
Being proactive means taking charge of your life. Leaders control events that are in there cicle of influence. They don’t blame circumstances; instead, they focus on what they can influence, therefore Proactive leaders set goals, prioritize, and take deliberate action.

Habit 2: Begin With the End in Mind
Effective leaders envision their desired outcomes. They create personal mission statements, aligning their actions with long-term goals. Whether you’re leading a team or your own life, knowing your destination ensures purposeful decisions.

Today we shall study these two habits next week we shall review more.
